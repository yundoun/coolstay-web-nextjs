## AccommodationCard 확장
- rating (⭐ 평점 + 리뷰 수)
- mileageRate (+N% 적립)
- hasCoupon (쿠폰 뱃지)
- address (주소 표시)
- reviewCount

## mapStoreToAccommodation 매핑 추가
- rating ← parseFloat(store.rating?.avg_score)
- reviewCount ← store.rating?.review_count
- mileageRate ← store.benefit_point_rate
- hasCoupon ← download_coupon_info.status !== "NON_TARGET"
- address ← store.location?.address
